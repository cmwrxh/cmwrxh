Energy-Forecaster

Predictive demand modeling for hyperscale data centers, matching rapid AI growth with clean energy sources including solar, battery storage, and SMR nuclear.

01 // Problem Statement

The global AI boom is driving a massive increase in power demand. Hyperscale data centers now require GW-scale energy solutions, but existing grids are often unable to keep pace with the 2026–2030 growth projections.

Brilliant Unicorn built the Energy-Forecaster to help operators model demand scenarios and identify the most resilient clean-energy mitigation paths.

02 // Technical Implementation

Demand Intelligence

By utilizing AlphaCore's validation layers, the system ingest massive datasets regarding compute clusters (GPU counts/TDP) and matches them against renewable energy availability curves to forecast grid-stability and potential brownout risks.
