Charles Mwaura

Systems engineer. Founder. Builder of dependable software systems for real business use.

Brilliant Unicorn is led by Charles Mwaura, a systems engineer with a background in mathematics and cyber security. The company was built around a simple idea: modern businesses need systems that are dependable, measurable, and structured for growth.

At Brilliant Unicorn, he led the technical design and delivery of automation and analytics systems used to support consulting and SaaS-style services. From 2020 to 2023, this included a data processing and analytics platform combining ingestion pipelines, validation layers, and reporting dashboards for client-facing data services.

Earlier work included building the AlphaCore Framework, a reusable orchestration layer supporting internal tools and service delivery systems. That background continues to shape the company’s approach to architecture, implementation, and product direction.

The Engineering Standard

Brilliant Unicorn is built on a simple belief: when the base system fails, everything on top of it becomes unstable.

Correctness Before Optimization

A fast system that produces unreliable outputs is still a broken system. Accuracy and integrity come first.

Resilience Before Scale

Scale only matters when the underlying system can withstand operational pressure without losing reliability.

Systems Over Noise

The goal is not complexity for its own sake, but dependable execution, clarity, and repeatable outcomes.
