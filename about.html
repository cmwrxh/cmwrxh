The Founder

Charles Mwaura

Systems Engineer. Founder.
MSc Cyber Security Candidate.
BSc Mathematics & IT.

Brilliant Unicorn was built around a simple idea: modern businesses need systems that are dependable, measurable, and structured for growth.

Led by Charles Mwaura, a systems engineer with a background in mathematics and cyber security, we bridge the gap between complex technical architecture and practical business execution.

Between 2020 and 2023, Charles led the technical design and delivery of internal automation and analytics systems used to support consulting and SaaS-style services. This included a comprehensive data processing platform combining ingestion pipelines, validation layers, and reporting dashboards.

Earlier work involved the development of the AlphaCore Framework, a reusable orchestration layer designed to streamline internal tools and service delivery systems. This foundation of technical ownership continues to drive our approach to building reliable, secure infrastructure.
