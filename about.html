Brilliant Unicorn is a founder-led company focused on automation systems, analytics platforms, and secure backend infrastructure built for reliability, clarity, and long-term business use.

The Company

Brilliant Unicorn was built around a simple idea: businesses should run on systems that are dependable, measurable, and structured for growth.

The company focuses on designing automation workflows, analytics systems, and technical infrastructure that help founders and organizations operate with more clarity and less friction.

The Founder

Brilliant Unicorn is led by Charles Mwaura, a systems engineer with a background in mathematics and cyber security.

From 2020 to 2023, he led the technical design and delivery of automation and analytics systems supporting consulting and SaaS-style services, including a data processing and analytics platform built around ingestion pipelines, validation layers, and reporting dashboards.
