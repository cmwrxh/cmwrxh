Data Protection

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

At Brilliant Unicorn LLC, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or purchase our digital products and services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide our services:

  • Name and email address (for account creation and order delivery)
  • Purchase history and product access information
  • Technical support communications (if you request implementation help)

We do not collect sensitive personal data such as payment card details — these are processed securely by our payment providers.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver your purchased digital frameworks and courses
  • To provide implementation support and customer service
  • To send important updates about your purchases
  • To improve our products and user experience

4. Third-Party Processors

We use trusted third-party services to process payments and deliver content:

  • Lemon Squeezy / Stripe – for payments and order fulfillment
  • Formspree – for newsletter and waitlist signups
  • Vercel – for website hosting

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and only use it for the services they provide to us.

Under the Kenya Data Protection Act 2019, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
