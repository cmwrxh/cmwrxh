"I build systems that don't break when the business grows."

I'm Charles Mwaura, a 30-year-old software and data engineer based in Nairobi, Kenya. With years of experience building production-grade systems, I founded Brilliant Unicorn Ltd to create reusable, battle-tested digital frameworks that ambitious founders can deploy instantly.

My core work revolves around the AlphaCore Framework — a high-performance orchestration layer designed for resilient automation, secure infrastructure, and scalable business systems. I now offer these frameworks as instant-access digital products, along with optional done-with-you implementation support for those who want faster, reliable results.
